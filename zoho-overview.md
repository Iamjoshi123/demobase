Zoho Invoice demo summary

Product
- Zoho Invoice provides invoicing, quotes, customer management, items, expenses, recurring invoices, payment links, and dashboard reporting.
- The public demo account is a no-login sample organization for India edition.
- The demo organization uses sample data and safe read-only walkthroughs are the target behavior for this demo.
