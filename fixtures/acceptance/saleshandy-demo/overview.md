# Saleshandy Demo Overview

Saleshandy is a sales engagement platform focused on outbound prospecting and follow-up.

Core areas available in the public demo:
- Sequences for multi-step outreach automation
- Reports for performance and analytics
- CRM list views for prospect management
- Unified Inbox for conversations
- Lead Finder and Client Management modules

The public demo starts at `/demo` and then redirects into the product experience with a demo token.
