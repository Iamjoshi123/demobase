# Acme CRM Pro Product Overview

Acme CRM Pro is a B2B customer relationship management platform built for mid-market sales teams that need one place to manage pipeline, contacts, and forecasting.

## Core Workflows

- Dashboard: Sales reps open the dashboard to review active deals, forecast updates, and recent activity.
- Contacts: Teams manage contact records with company, title, email, phone, and tags.
- Deals: Managers track pipeline stages, deal values, and owner performance.
- Reporting: RevOps teams review sales overview, revenue forecast, and activity reports.

## Integrations

Acme CRM Pro supports Gmail, Outlook, Slack notifications, calendar sync, and Zapier or webhook-based automation.

## Demo Positioning

Buyer demos focus on day-to-day sales workflows, reporting visibility, and ease of onboarding new reps.
