# Reporting And Analytics

## Built-In Reports

Acme CRM Pro ships with Sales Overview, Revenue Forecast, Team Performance, and Activity Report dashboards.

## Custom Reports

Revenue leaders can create a custom report by selecting a data source, choosing metrics and dimensions, applying filters, and saving the result as a dashboard widget or scheduled email report.

## Dashboard Widgets

The analytics page supports drag-and-drop widgets, auto-refresh intervals, and CSV or PDF export for leadership reviews.
