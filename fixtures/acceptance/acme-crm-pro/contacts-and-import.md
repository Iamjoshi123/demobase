# Contacts And CSV Import

## Creating A Contact

Sales reps create a contact by opening Contacts, selecting New Contact, and entering First Name, Last Name, and Email. The form also supports Company, Title, Phone, Address, and tags so the record can be routed and segmented immediately.

## Linking A Contact To A Company

While creating or editing the contact, the rep can attach the contact to an existing company record or create a new company relationship during the same workflow.

## CSV Import

Contacts can be imported or exported via CSV from the Contacts page. Admins typically go to Contacts, choose Import, upload the CSV file, map columns, and confirm the import preview before committing changes.
