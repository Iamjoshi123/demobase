Generate custom reports with charts and exports.

## FAQ

**Q: How do I create a new contact?**
A: Navigate to Contacts > Click "New Contact" > Fill in the form > Save.

**Q: Can I import contacts from CSV?**
A: Yes, go to Contacts > Import > Upload CSV file.

**Q: How do I customize my dashboard?**
A: Click the gear icon on the dashboard and drag widgets to rearrange them.
